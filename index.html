Frequently asked questions

Quick answers. If you still have questions, we’ll help.

What stage do I need to be at to join?

Early-stage is fine. The key is that you’re actively building and ready to make decisions week to week.

What if I miss a live session?

You’ll still get the templates and weekly build prompts, and we can help you catch up in office hours.

Is this a course or more hands-on?

Hands-on. The goal is to ship real assets and an operating rhythm you can keep using.

How much time should I budget per week?

Typically 2–4 hours total: the live session plus a short weekly build sprint.
